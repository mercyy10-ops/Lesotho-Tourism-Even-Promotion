Target Tourists and Locals

This event is designed to bring together people from all walks of life — both within Lesotho and across the world. It celebrates the nation’s cultural identity, musical heritage, and community spirit.

  • Global Tourists
  • Local People and Families
  • Music Enthusiasts
  • Concertgoers
  • Cultural Explorers

Whether you are visiting Lesotho for the first time or you are a proud Mosotho, this event offers a unique opportunity to experience the beauty, rhythm, and unity of our culture.
